Wind, solar and batteries are poised to enable the power sector to meet its share of emission cuts required under the Paris climate agreement, at least until 2030, according to BNEF. Coal will be the biggest loser in the power sector, with its share of global generation plunging from 37% today to 12% in 2050, BNEF said.<br/>Those other renewables could include geothermal systems, fuel cells and devices that harvest energy from ocean waves and tides.
