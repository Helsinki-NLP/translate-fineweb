| Mycotoxins are one of the most dangerous hidden threats to livestock health, compromising growth, fertility, disease immunity and overall performance.<br/>Moulds produce mycotoxins as a response to environmental stress conditions, so shifts in temperature or rainfall can lead to their occurrence. Poor handling and storage conditions can further contaminate feeds. They can occur at every stage of production, from harvesting, ensiling, storage and compounding feeds.
