The installation will be greatly simplified if you add a run of empty conduit going up to the roof of the house. Then, when you are ready to "go solar", the electrician's work will be much easier as the wiring can be routed through this pre-exhisting conduit. Not only is it less expensive to install this conduit during the construction phase, it will save you the trouble of having to break through the attic and walls to do the wiring after the fact. With a properly-sized system, you will generate more electricity than you use, thus potentially zeroing-out your electric bill. My home, like many photovoltaic- powered homes, uses a bank of batteries to store excess electricity.
