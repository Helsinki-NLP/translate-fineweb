In fact post-WWI studies ascertained that had the British adopted the German style, head injuries could be minimised by up to 50 per cent! It wasn't done, by the way as it was seen to be "not British" to follow the German example - regardless of how many lives it may have saved!<br/>In this possibly apocryphal anecdote, however, lies an important grain of truth.
