An escape hatch opens from the engine room into the aft compartment, whilst a second escape hatch exits via the fore cabin roof.<br/><br/>The lifeboat is also equipped with two basket stretchers and a Neil Robertson stretcher, first aid equipment, a salvage pump, emergency rations, fire hose and general service pump, a small inflatable rowing boat, fire axe, blankets, night vision equipment, searchlights, breeches buoy, flares and rocket lines.
