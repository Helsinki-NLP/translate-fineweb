|  Phill Jupitus Experiment. Sounds avant-garde, doesn't it? Well, first-off, don't be fooled. They walk in, are given a random scenario and ten seconds to change their clothes then be subjected to a roughly-ten-minute cross-examination by The Voice. If any of his answers displease The Voice, they will be subjected to a short beep and forced to come up with an alternative.<br/><br/>So far, so 'Should Have Said' but tonight, the results were distinctly mixed as Phill Jupitus took to the stage. With some situations, such as the swashbuckling cinema usher or the aging film producer, the character clicked immediately and proved fertile ground for some entertaining banter with The Voice.
