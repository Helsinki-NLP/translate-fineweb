Keep emphasizing the straightening of your legs in order to get the maximum release out of the hamstrings. If the tight hamstrings are painful let the legs move gently in a "flutter kick" which still activates the stretch but relieves the pain. | Description: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a synchronous serial interface technology proposed by Motorola. It is a high-speed, full-duplex, synchronous communication bus that occupies only four pins in the chip. Control and data transfer, widely used in EEPROM, Flash, RTC (real time clock), ADC (digital to analog converter), DSP (digital signal processor) and digital signal decoder. The speed of SPI communication can easily reach several megabits, so you can use the SPI bus to transfer some uncompressed audio and compressed video. | Descripción: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) es una tecnología de interfaz serie síncrona propuesta por Motorola.
