The WHIM in the wall should absorb some of the X-rays from the AGN as they make their journey across intergalactic space to Earth.<br/><br/>Using new data from Chandra and previous observations with both Chandra and XMM-Newton, absorption of X-rays by oxygen atoms in the WHIM has clearly been detected by Fang and his colleagues.
