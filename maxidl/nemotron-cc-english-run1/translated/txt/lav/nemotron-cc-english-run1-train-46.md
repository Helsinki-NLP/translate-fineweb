The cysts formed in the kidneys are similar to those formed in other parts of the body. In most of the cases, the cysts are fluid filled and lead to abnormal functioning of the kidneys. This disorder of the kidneys is commonly observed in older adults (above 50 years of age).<br/><br/>The exact cause of kidney cysts is unknown; however, certain factors like heredity and genetics are believed to the reasons for the same. In case of simple kidney cyst, only a single cyst is formed; whereas, in case of Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD), more than one or multiple cysts are formed. The condition can be divided into two types: simple and complex cases. Simple kidney cysts hardly lead to kidney cancer. On the contrary, there are high chances of a complex cases leading to kidney cancer. On the other hand, people who have suffered from urinary tract infections in the past are more prone to suffer from kidney cyst. | The Ottoman Empire started its westward expansion into Europe in the middle of the 14th century. Its first significant opponent was the young Serbian Empire, which was worn down by a series of campaigns, notably in the Battle of Kosovo in 1389, in which the leaders of both armies were killed, and which gained a central role in Serbian folklore as an epic battle and beginning of bad luck for Serbia. More than forty-five thousand Ottoman troops, including four pashas, have been reported as part of the Ottoman casualties. At the same time, the Ottoman Empire started sea campaigns, as early as 1423, when it waged a seven year war with the Venetian Republic over maritime control of the Aegean and the Adriatic.
