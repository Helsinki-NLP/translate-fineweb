| The play "Equus" by Peter Schaffer investigates ideas of faith, passion, violence, and adolescent sexuality. Equus is a fictional account of what Schaffer believes could have happened before the incident, helping to explain the psychology and reasoning behind the boy's mysterious and disturbing crime. Equus follows closely the character of 17 - year-old boy Alan Strang, but also his psychiatrist, Martin Dysart.
