Sometimes, due to certain reasons, abnormal fluid or air-filled sacs are formed in the kidneys. These are known as kidney cysts. The cysts formed in the kidneys are similar to those formed in other parts of the body. In most of the cases, the cysts are fluid filled and lead to abnormal functioning of the kidneys. This disorder of the kidneys is commonly observed in older adults (above 50 years of age).<br/><br/>The exact cause of kidney cysts is unknown; however, certain factors like heredity and genetics are believed to the reasons for the same. In case of simple kidney cyst, only a single cyst is formed; whereas, in case of Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD), more than one or multiple cysts are formed. The condition can be divided into two types: simple and complex cases. Simple kidney cysts hardly lead to kidney cancer. On the contrary, there are high chances of a complex cases leading to kidney cancer. Secondly, these cysts can rupture, leading to severe complications.<br/><br/>Symptoms of Kidney Cyst<br/><br/>Symptoms of kidney cyst vary according to the type. One should note that a majority of people over 50 years of age suffer from it, but, are unaware of it. On the other hand, people who have suffered from urinary tract infections in the past are more prone to suffer from kidney cyst. More than forty-five thousand Ottoman troops, including four pashas, have been reported as part of the Ottoman casualties. At the same time, the Ottoman Empire started sea campaigns, as early as 1423, when it waged a seven year war with the Venetian Republic over maritime control of the Aegean and the Adriatic.
