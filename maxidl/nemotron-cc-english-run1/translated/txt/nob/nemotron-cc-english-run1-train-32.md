An example would be when a person is upset with the someone who inadvertently walked in front of them at the grocery store and punched that someone for doing so.<br/><br/>Product liability: The act of selling a defective product or placing a defective product in the stream of commerce which leads to injury of the consumer.<br/><br/>At The White Law Group, it is our goal and primary objective to obtain relief for our clients for the damages they have incurred and deter those in the wrong from committing their tortious acts in the future.
