| Description: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a synchronous serial interface technology proposed by Motorola. It is a high-speed, full-duplex, synchronous communication bus that occupies only four pins in the chip. Control and data transfer, widely used in EEPROM, Flash, RTC (real time clock), ADC (digital to analog converter), DSP (digital signal processor) and digital signal decoder. The speed of SPI communication can easily reach several megabits, so you can use the SPI bus to transfer some uncompressed audio and compressed video. | Deskribapena: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) Motorolak proposatutako serieko interfazearen teknologia sinkronoa da. Abiadura handiko banda zabal eta sinkrono bat da, txipean lau pin baino ez dituena. Kontrol eta datuen transferentzia, asko erabiltzen da EEPROM, Flash, RTC (denbora errealeko erlojua), ADC (seinale digitalaren prozesadorea), DSP (seinale digitalaren prozesadorea) eta seinale digitalaren dekodea.
