| Description: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a synchronous serial interface technology proposed by Motorola. It is a high-speed, full-duplex, synchronous communication bus that occupies only four pins in the chip. Control and data transfer, widely used in EEPROM, Flash, RTC (real time clock), ADC (digital to analog converter), DSP (digital signal processor) and digital signal decoder. The speed of SPI communication can easily reach several megabits, so you can use the SPI bus to transfer some uncompressed audio and compressed video. | Opis: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) je tehnologija sinhronega serijskega vmesnika, ki jo predlaga Motorola. Gre za visokohitrostno, polno-duplexno, sinhrono komunikacijsko vodilo, ki zavzema le štiri pine v čipu. Nadzor in prenos podatkov, ki se pogosto uporablja v EEPROM, Flash, RTC (v realnem času ura), ADC (digitalni analogni pretvornik), DSP (digitalni procesor signala) in digitalni signal dekoder. Hitrost komunikacije SPI lahko zlahka doseže več megabitov, tako da lahko uporabite SPI avtobus za prenos nekaj nestisnjenega zvoka in stisnjenega videa. |
