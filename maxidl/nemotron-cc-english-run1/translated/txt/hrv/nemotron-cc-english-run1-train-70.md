| Description: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a synchronous serial interface technology proposed by Motorola. It is a high-speed, full-duplex, synchronous communication bus that occupies only four pins in the chip. Control and data transfer, widely used in EEPROM, Flash, RTC (real time clock), ADC (digital to analog converter), DSP (digital signal processor) and digital signal decoder. The speed of SPI communication can easily reach several megabits, so you can use the SPI bus to transfer some uncompressed audio and compressed video. | Opis: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) je tehnologija sinkronog serijskog sučelja koju predlaže Motorola. To je brza, potpuno dupleksna, sinkrona komunikacijska sabirnica koja zauzima samo četiri pina u čipu. Kontrola i prijenos podataka, naširoko koristi u EEPROM, Flash, RTC (sat u stvarnom vremenu), ADC (digitalni analogni pretvarač), DSP (digitalni procesor signala) i digitalni dekoder signala. Brzina SPI komunikacije može lako doseći nekoliko megabita, tako da možete koristiti SPI sabirnicu za prijenos nekomprimiranog zvuka i komprimiranog videozapisa. |
