| Description: SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a synchronous serial interface technology proposed by Motorola. Control and data transfer, widely used in EEPROM, Flash, RTC (real time clock), ADC (digital to analog converter), DSP (digital signal processor) and digital signal decoder.
