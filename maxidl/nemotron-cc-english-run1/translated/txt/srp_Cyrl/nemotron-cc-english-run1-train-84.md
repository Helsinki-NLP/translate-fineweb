To put that number in perspective, in the U.S. 309 people in every 100,000 are doctors; for Native Americans, that number is 20. There are 83,000 faculty in U.S. medical schools; 140 are Native American. The total number of applicants to U.S. medical schools last year was 42,000; only 415 self-identified as Native Americans. Of that 415, it is not known how many actually got into medical school.<br/><br/>"I believe this scholarship program can do some good for Indian people.
