We asked 30 pairs of people to build structures out of blocks, limiting their communication to either Gesture Only, Speech Only, or Gesture and Speech. We found differences in how gesture and speech were used to communicate across the three conditions and found that pairs in the Gesture and Speech condition completed tasks faster than those in Speech Only.
