If you are trying to stop drinking alcohol but your entirely social life exists in a bar, then you may have to re-examine this aspect of your social life. It is important to develop a plan or strategy to deal with these scenarios if you are going to be successful in your behavior change.<br/><br/>The Institutional, or Organizational, level is made up of those organized groups to which we belong.
