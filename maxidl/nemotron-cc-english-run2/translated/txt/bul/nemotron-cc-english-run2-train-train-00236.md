The lower first premolar is often missing bilaterally. Every time a tooth appears to be missing, dental radiographs are needed to rule out embedded teeth. Embedded teeth may result in the formation of oral cysts (such as the dentigerous cyst), which may be locally destructive and lead to serious consequences.<br/><br/>Chronic obstructive respiratory syndrome (CORS) of brachycephalic breeds has been described.
