"The context of the artifact really tells us the story. It tells us how old they are, what they were used for. | The Power of Making a Difference<br/><br/>Creating a legacy and making your mark.<br/><br/>Parents basically want two things for their children: they want them to be happy, and they want them to be good. It's a power hitter hitting a home run.<br/><br/>Imagine you were able to bring peace to the Middle East. It is not that making your mark in the world is better than integrity; rather, it incorporates integrity and brings it home.<br/><br/>But power for power's sake — that's destruction.
