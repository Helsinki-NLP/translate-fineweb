| No Summer Slump!<br/><br/>The Need to Read – Resisting Summer Learning Loss<br/><br/>Only a few students welcome summer as a time to relax with a stack of books. Fishing is highly valued, and instead of sending their children to school to receive an education they teach their children how to fish. By the age of 10, most children can already catch small fish which they sell and get money for.
