Since its organisation in 1735, it has also been the archives of the Cis-Tisza Reformed Church District. The family archives are rich, as well as the collection related to the economic history of Sárospatak.<br/>The repository collection contains valuable documents for researchers dealing with local history, church history, spiritual ethnography and the life of Hungarians.<br/>Initially, it collected material on the villages of the Bodrogköz, the Hegyköz and the Hegyalja, but now it contains documents on the Hungarian population of the entire Carpathian Basin. | We present a detailed study of the nonlinear thermoelectric properties of a molecular junction, represented by a dissipative Anderson-Holstein model. A single-orbital level with strong Coulomb interaction is coupled to a localized vibrational mode and we account for both electron and phonon exchange with both electrodes, investigating how these contribute to the heat and charge transports. We calculate the efficiency and power output of the device operated as a heat to electric power converter in the regime of weak tunnel coupling and phonon exchange rate and identify the optimal operating conditions, which are found to be qualitatively changed by the presence of the vibrational mode. Based on this study of a generic model system, we discuss the desirable properties of molecular junctions for thermoelectric applications. | Táimid i láthair le staidéar mionsonraithe ar na hairíonna teirmeach nonlinear de acomhal móilíneach, a bhfuil samhail díspeagtha Anderson-Holstein. Tá leibhéal aon-dhíorrach le hidirghníomhú láidir Coulomb comhcheangailte le modh chreathadh áitiúil agus cuntas againn ar mhalartú leictreonach agus teileafóin araon le leictreoidí, ag déanamh imscrúdaithe ar an gcaoi a gcuireann siad seo le hiompar teasa agus muirir. Ríomhaimid éifeachtúlacht agus aschur cumhachta na feiste a oibrítear mar thiontán cumhachta teasa chuig tiontaire cumhachta leictreach i réimeas cúplála tollán lag agus ráta malairte an fhóna agus na coinníollacha oibríochta is fearr a aithint, a fhaightear amach go n-athrófar go cáilíochtúil trí láithreacht an mhodh chreathadh. Bunaithe ar an staidéar seo ar chóras múnla cineálach, pléimid ar airíonna inmhianaithe acomhail mhóilíneacha le haghaidh feidhmeanna teirmiméadairiciúla. |
