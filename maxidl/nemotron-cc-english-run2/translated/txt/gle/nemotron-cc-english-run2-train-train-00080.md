By dropping their leaves, the tree can prevent water loss from photosynthesis and transpiration that normally takes place in the leaves. Trees also conserve energy during this dormant period since they do not have to protect the leaves from freezing.<br/><br/>Reproductive Adaptation<br/><br/>Maple trees reproduce by generating seed-bearing fruit called samaras.
