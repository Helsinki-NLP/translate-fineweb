That's because a VPN can change your IP address to fool a server into believing that you're located in a different country, but a geolocation check happens on the device itself (usually using GPS), and therefore can't be spoofed in the same way.<br/><br/>Apple might use other ways to check your location, and it already has a system in place that does just this.
