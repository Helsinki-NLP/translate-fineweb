The port of Dieppe was open and the ports of Ostend, Boulogne and Antwerp were being made ready to accept shipping.<br/><br/>The HQ of the Transportation Stores organisation moved to Brussels and controlled four Transportation Stores Depots and two Transportation Spares Depot. It was necessary to repair the rail system East of the Rhine in order to operate a railhead and railtail organisation until a railway bridge across the Rhine could be constructed. The site for the Rhine bridge was at Spyk and work commenced on 7 April.
