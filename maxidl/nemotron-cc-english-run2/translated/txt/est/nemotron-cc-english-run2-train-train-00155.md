| Primate<br/><br/>PRIMATE (from Low Lat. primas = one who held the first place, primas partes). During the 4th and 5th centuries A.D. the title was<br/>applied to both secular and ecclesiastical officials. The Theodosian Code mentions primates of towns, districts and fortified places (Primates urbium, vicorum,<br/>castellorum) . The Pragmatic Sanction of Justinian also mentions primates governing a district, primates regionis; and in this sense the title survived, under<br/>Turkish rule, in Greece until the 1pth century. An official called " primate of the palace " is mentioned in the laws of the Visigoths. Primas also seems to<br/>have been used loosely during the middle ages for " head " or " chief." Du Cange cites primas castri. The title, however, has been more generally used to denote<br/>a bishop with special privileges and powers. It was first employed almost synonymously with metropolitan to denote the chief bishop of a province having his see<br/>in the capital and certain rights of superintendence over the whole province. At the Council of Nicaea (A.D. 325) the metropolitan constitution was assumed as<br/>universal, and after this the terms " metropolitan," and " primate," to denote the chief bishop of a province, came into general use. The title of primate was<br/>used more generally in Africa, while elsewhere metropolitan was more generally employed. The primates in Africa differed from those elsewhere in that the title<br/>always belonged to the longest ordained bishop in a province, who had not necessarily his see in the capital, except in the case of the bishop of Carthage, who<br/>was head also of the other five African provinces. There were also three sorts of honorary primates: (i) primates aevo, the oldest bishop in a province next to<br/>the primate, on whom power devolved when the primate was disabled or disqualified; (2) titular metropolitans, the bishops of certain cities which had the name<br/>and title of civil metropoles bestowed on them by some emperor; (3) the bishops of some mother-churches which were honoured by ancient custom but were subject<br/>to the ordinary metropolitan, e.g. the bishop of Jerusalem, who was subject to his metropolitan at Caesarea.<br/><br/>At a later date " primate " became the official title of certain metropolitans who obtained from the pope a position of episcopal authority over several<br/>other metropolitans and who were, at the same time, appointed vicars of the Holy See. This was done in the case of the bishops of Aries and Thessalonica as<br/>early as the 5th century. Such primates were sometimes also called patriarchs, primates diocesearum (political, not episcopal dioceses), primates provinciae,<br/>summi primates, praesules omnium sacerdotum in partibus suis. In this sense the Western primate was considered the equivalent of the Eastern patriarch. The<br/>archbishop of Reims received the title of primas inter primates. By the False Decretals an attempt was made to establish such a primacy as a permanent<br/>institution, but the attempt was not successful and the dignity of primate became more or less honorary. The overlapping of the title is illustrated by the case<br/>of England, where the archbishop of York still bears the title of primate of England and the archbishop of Canterbury that of primate of all England. A less<br/>general use of the title is its application in medieval usage to the head of a cathedral school or college (primas scholarum) and to the dignitaries of a<br/>cathedral church. The abbot of Fulda received from the pope the title of primas inter abbales. Keegi nagu Taylor Swift võiks proovida laiendada oma ülemaailmset haaret, pakkudes oma uut albumit tasuta ainult nendele riikidele, kus ta pole veel suur staar, sundides teisi maksma tellimuste eest, kui nad seda tõesti tahavad. See võib olla tema jaoks hea turundustaktika, kuid see võib voogesituse teenuse jaoks väga kergesti tagasi lüüa, muutes kogu süsteemi ebaõiglaseks.<br/><br/>Kui see uks on avatud, oleks seda kõik peaaegu võimatu sulgeda. Lubades Swiftil oma teed viia teiste võimsate nimedeni, kes üritavad teha sama, ja sealt on voogesituse teenus avatud. Spotify tegevjuht Daniel Ek tegi raske valiku, kui ta Taylorile ei ütles, ja kuigi ma olen kindel, et ettevõte ei armastaks midagi rohkem kui tema muusika kättesaadavust, on tõenäoliselt tema äri huvides tema nõudmiste keeldumine.<br/><br/>Kuigi ettevõte ei ole kasumlik, on Spotify jaoks asjad seni üsna hästi, mis on kiiresti muutumas maailma muusika juurde. Vaatamata sellele, et sellel aastal ei ole maailma suurim album, on uued kasutajad registreerunud, nii et on ebatõenäoline, et muuta oma seisukohta, et kunstnikel on õigus oma töö ligipääsetavuse üle sõna võtta. Loomulikult võivad nad siiski Spotify'i täielikult vältida, kuid paljude jaoks on see rohkem ebasoodsas olukorras kui võit. Müügi vähenemisel muutub voogesitus üha olulisemaks kõigi muusikute jaoks ja Spotify väidab, et nende tasuta tase on oluline, et veenda kasutajaid lõpuks maksma.<br/><br/>Ideaalses maailmas oleks kunstnikel vabadus levitada oma teoseid nii, nagu nad tahavad, kus nad tahavad ja kui palju nad tahavad, kuid kahjuks, kuna muusikatööstus on ikka veel välja mõtlemas, kuidas jääda ellu nüüd, kui paljud ei tunne enam vajadust osta tooteid, mis lihtsalt ei ole praegu nii. Võib-olla, kui rohkem inimesi on voogesitusega pardale tulnud ja selle eest maksavad, võib olla veidi liikumisruumi, kuid praegu tundub, et see on Spotify viis või maantee. |
