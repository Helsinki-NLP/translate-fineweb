The ADA definition of disability is "a physical or mental impairment that substantially limits one of more major life activities." This is a legal as opposed to medical definition. The Kurt Vonnegut Memorial Library offered to sponsor the garden.<br/><br/>The Michigan Hemingway Society agreed to donate a mint plant that Ernest Hemingway mentions in two of his stories. Science classes, for example, will be able to study the plants.<br/><br/>"It's really nice to see a lot of students show a devotion to the class. … Miss McQuillan works really hard on these things and tries to make a lasting impact on the school," Strager said.<br/><br/>The garden is multidisciplinary in that different departments can take advantage of it. For instance, the environmental science class volunteered to test the soil during the school year, McQuillan said.<br/><br/>"We really want to make it something the kids feel invested in no matter what class they're taking.
