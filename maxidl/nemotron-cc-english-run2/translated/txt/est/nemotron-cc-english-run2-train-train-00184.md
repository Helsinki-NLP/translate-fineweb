| WHAT IS THE GOSPEL?<br/><br/>The gospel is the good news about God's Son, Jesus Christ, our Lord. The gospel defines and unites the people of God. The gospel is an exclusive message in that it expresses the only hope of salvation; yet, the gospel is also an inclusive message in that it offers hope for all people. This gospel was promised and testified to in the Old Testament and is fulfilled, proclaimed, and explained in the New Testament.
