Since its organisation in 1735, it has also been the archives of the Cis-Tisza Reformed Church District. The family archives are rich, as well as the collection related to the economic history of Sárospatak.<br/>The repository collection contains valuable documents for researchers dealing with local history, church history, spiritual ethnography and the life of Hungarians.<br/>Initially, it collected material on the villages of the Bodrogköz, the Hegyköz and the Hegyalja, but now it contains documents on the Hungarian population of the entire Carpathian Basin.
