Furthermore, while calculation skills are often most impacted, mathematical reasoning, applying mathematical concepts and procedures, are also areas of difficulty.<br/><br/>Dyscalculia is a lifelong condition and changes in symptomatology happen with age, resulting in a shifting or persistent array of difficulties across the lifespan.
