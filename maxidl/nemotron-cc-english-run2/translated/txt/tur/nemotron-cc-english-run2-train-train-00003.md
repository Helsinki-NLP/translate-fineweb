This handicap is more in male calves than in females. Why? Males need to be big and strong as they reach adulthood in order to win mates. This means his chances of winning a battle for a mate are lesser.<br/><br/>But there is a reprieve. But the big elephants are also the older, wiser, important mentors in a herd.<br/><br/>In a scenario where matriarchs and females are poached, babies are left orphaned and herds lose their biggest and strongest members.
