The risk of impermanence of afforestation and reforestation CDM projects has so far been addressed by issuing temporary credits which expire and need to be replaced with permanent credits.<br/><br/>Given that this approach has been highly unattractive to investors, some countries have proposed new approaches to address non-permanence which will be discussed in Lima.
