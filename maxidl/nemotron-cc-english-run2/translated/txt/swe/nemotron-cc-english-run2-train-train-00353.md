| Archives<br/><br/>Friday, October 1, 2021<br/><br/>The Archives hold some 460 linear metres of documents and records, which from 1294 onwards are an indispensable source for the ecclesiastical, social and cultural history of Northern Hungary. Since its organisation in 1735, it has also been the archives of the Cis-Tisza Reformed Church District. The family archives are rich, as well as the collection related to the economic history of Sárospatak.<br/>The repository collection contains valuable documents for researchers dealing with local history, church history, spiritual ethnography and the life of Hungarians.<br/>Initially, it collected material on the villages of the Bodrogköz, the Hegyköz and the Hegyalja, but now it contains documents on the Hungarian population of the entire Carpathian Basin. | We present a detailed study of the nonlinear thermoelectric properties of a molecular junction, represented by a dissipative Anderson-Holstein model. A single-orbital level with strong Coulomb interaction is coupled to a localized vibrational mode and we account for both electron and phonon exchange with both electrodes, investigating how these contribute to the heat and charge transports. We calculate the efficiency and power output of the device operated as a heat to electric power converter in the regime of weak tunnel coupling and phonon exchange rate and identify the optimal operating conditions, which are found to be qualitatively changed by the presence of the vibrational mode. Based on this study of a generic model system, we discuss the desirable properties of molecular junctions for thermoelectric applications. | Vi presenterar en detaljerad studie av de icke-linjära termoelektriska egenskaperna hos en molekylär korsning, representerad av en dissipativ Anderson-Holstein-modell. En singel-orbital jämnar med starkt Coulomb växelverkan kopplas till en lokaliserad vibrational funktionsläge, och vi redogör för både elektronen och fononutbytet med båda elektroderna och undersöker hur dessa bidrar till värme- och laddningtransporterna. Vi beräknar effektiviteten och uteffekten av enheten som drivs som en värme till elektrisk kraftomvandlare i systemet med svag tunnelkoppling och fononväxelkurs och identifierar de optimala driftsförhållandena, som finns att vara kvalitativt förändrade av närvaron av vibrationsläget. Baserat på denna studie av ett generiskt modellsystem diskuterar vi de önskvärda egenskaperna hos molekylära korsningar för termoelektriska applikationer. |
