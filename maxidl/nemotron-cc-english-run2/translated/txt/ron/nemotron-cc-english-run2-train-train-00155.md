| Primate<br/><br/>PRIMATE (from Low Lat. primas = one who held the first place, primas partes). During the 4th and 5th centuries A.D. the title was<br/>applied to both secular and ecclesiastical officials. The Theodosian Code mentions primates of towns, districts and fortified places (Primates urbium, vicorum,<br/>castellorum) . The Pragmatic Sanction of Justinian also mentions primates governing a district, primates regionis; and in this sense the title survived, under<br/>Turkish rule, in Greece until the 1pth century. An official called " primate of the palace " is mentioned in the laws of the Visigoths. Primas also seems to<br/>have been used loosely during the middle ages for " head " or " chief." Du Cange cites primas castri. The title, however, has been more generally used to denote<br/>a bishop with special privileges and powers. It was first employed almost synonymously with metropolitan to denote the chief bishop of a province having his see<br/>in the capital and certain rights of superintendence over the whole province. At the Council of Nicaea (A.D. 325) the metropolitan constitution was assumed as<br/>universal, and after this the terms " metropolitan," and " primate," to denote the chief bishop of a province, came into general use. The title of primate was<br/>used more generally in Africa, while elsewhere metropolitan was more generally employed. The primates in Africa differed from those elsewhere in that the title<br/>always belonged to the longest ordained bishop in a province, who had not necessarily his see in the capital, except in the case of the bishop of Carthage, who<br/>was head also of the other five African provinces. There were also three sorts of honorary primates: (i) primates aevo, the oldest bishop in a province next to<br/>the primate, on whom power devolved when the primate was disabled or disqualified; (2) titular metropolitans, the bishops of certain cities which had the name<br/>and title of civil metropoles bestowed on them by some emperor; (3) the bishops of some mother-churches which were honoured by ancient custom but were subject<br/>to the ordinary metropolitan, e.g. the bishop of Jerusalem, who was subject to his metropolitan at Caesarea.<br/><br/>At a later date " primate " became the official title of certain metropolitans who obtained from the pope a position of episcopal authority over several<br/>other metropolitans and who were, at the same time, appointed vicars of the Holy See. This was done in the case of the bishops of Aries and Thessalonica as<br/>early as the 5th century. Such primates were sometimes also called patriarchs, primates diocesearum (political, not episcopal dioceses), primates provinciae,<br/>summi primates, praesules omnium sacerdotum in partibus suis. In this sense the Western primate was considered the equivalent of the Eastern patriarch. The<br/>archbishop of Reims received the title of primas inter primates. By the False Decretals an attempt was made to establish such a primacy as a permanent<br/>institution, but the attempt was not successful and the dignity of primate became more or less honorary. Deși nu are cel mai mare album din lume în acest an, noii utilizatori se înscriu în grupuri, deci este puțin probabil să-și schimbe poziția privind acordarea artiștilor dreptul de a avea un cuvânt de spus cu privire la accesibilitatea operei lor. Desigur, ei pot alege să evite Spotify în întregime, dar pentru mulți, acest lucru se dovedește a fi mai mult un dezavantaj decât o victorie. Pe măsură ce vânzările scad, streaming-ul devine din ce în ce mai important pentru toți muzicienii, iar Spotify susține că nivelul lor gratuit este esențial pentru a convinge utilizatorii să se înregistreze ca clienți plătitori.<br/><br/>Într-o lume ideală, artiștilor li s-ar permite libertatea de a-și distribui munca așa cum doresc, unde doresc și pentru cât de mult doresc, dar din păcate, deoarece industria muzicală încă își dă seama cum să rămână în viață acum că mulți nu mai simt nevoia de a cumpăra produse, acest lucru nu este cazul acum. Poate că atunci când mai mulți oameni au venit la bord cu streaming și plătesc pentru ea ar putea exista o anumită marjă de manevră, dar pentru moment, se pare că este calea Spotify sau autostrada. |
