Coffee consumption is also associated with a decreased risk of heart disease and cirrhosis of the liver, according to a 2006 article published in the "New York Times." Studies specifically on espresso beans have not been conducted, but the beans contain more healthful components that are lost when they are turned into coffee.<br/><br/>Mood<br/><br/>Chocolate-covered espresso beans might improve your mood because both coffee and chocolate impact how you feel.
