| Get the Facts About Fruit Juice<br/><br/>Straight from the American Academy of Pediatrics<br/><br/>By Katherine Kopp<br/><br/>Published: January 30, 2019<br/><br/>Photo courtesy of Africa Studio/Shutterstock.com<br/><br/>According to the American Academy of Pediatrics, the average American drinks 6.6 gallons of juice each year and more than half of preschool children ages 2-5 drink juice regularly, consuming an average of 10 ounces per day. That is more than twice the amount of juice recommended by the American Academy of Pediatrics.<br/><br/>Over-consumption of juices can lead to obesity, which now affects 40 percent of adults and 19 percent of children in the U.S. Children ages 2-18 consume more than half of their fruit intake in the form of juice. Children and adults who drink juice feel less full than if they consume whole fruit, which can lead to an overall increase in food consumption.<br/><br/>Also, many fruit juices contain a significant amount of sugar. For example, a 12-ounce glass of orange juice contains 10 teaspoons of sugar, about the same as a can of Coca-Cola. Kids who drink a lot of juice tend to drink more sugary beverages, such as soft drinks, later on in life.<br/><br/>The amount of juice children are drinking has declined some in recent years, as more beverages such as flavored water gain popularity. The American Academy of Pediatrics recommends that parents and caregivers offer children whole fruits every day, instead of relying on fruit juices. | Aflați mai multe despre sucul de fructe<br/><br/>Direct de la Academia Americană de Pediatrie<br/><br/>De Katherine Kopp<br/><br/>Publicat: 30 ianuarie 2019<br/><br/>Fotografie oferită de Africa Studio/Shutterstock.com<br/><br/>Potrivit Academiei Americane de Pediatrie, un american bea in medie 6,6 galoane de suc in fiecare an si mai mult de jumatate dintre copiii prescolari cu varsta cuprinsa intre 2-5 ani beau suc in mod regulat, consumand in medie 10 uncii pe zi. Aceasta este mai mult de două ori cantitatea de suc recomandată de Academia Americană de Pediatrie.<br/><br/>Consumul excesiv de sucuri poate duce la obezitate, care afectează acum 40% dintre adulți și 19% dintre copiii din SUA. Copiii cu vârste cuprinse între 2-18 ani consumă mai mult de jumătate din aportul lor de fructe sub formă de suc. Copiii și adulții care beau suc se simt mai puțin plini decât dacă consumă fructe întregi, ceea ce poate duce la o creștere generală a consumului de alimente.<br/><br/>De asemenea, multe sucuri de fructe conțin o cantitate semnificativă de zahăr. De exemplu, un pahar de 12 uncii de suc de portocale conține 10 lingurițe de zahăr, aproximativ la fel ca o cutie de Coca-Cola. Copiii care beau mult suc tind să bea mai multe băuturi zaharoase, cum ar fi băuturile răcoritoare, mai târziu în viață.<br/><br/>Cantitatea de suc pe care copiii o beau a scăzut în ultimii ani, deoarece mai multe băuturi, cum ar fi apa aromatizată, câștigă popularitate. Academia Americana de Pediatrie recomanda ca parintii si ingrijitorii sa ofere copiilor fructe integrale in fiecare zi, in loc sa se bazeze pe sucuri de fructe. |
| Teaching the Ministry of Sound<br/><br/>The three-year Bachelor of Science qualification will focus on the engineering science of sound and music.<br/><br/>The programme, accredited by the Council on Higher Education, was designed according to National Qualification Framework Level 7 engineering technologist programmes offered by public universities.<br/><br/>Students who wish to become professionals in the technical audio industry will gain sufficient grounding in audio science, design and synthesis to produce innovative technical developments in the field.<br/><br/>Academy MD Tim Kraft said, "From the first day on campus, students are exposed to the entertainment industry and interact daily with industry professionals. With a collective experience of over 100 years, our lecturers are professionals in their own right and are highly respected in the industry."<br/><br/>Kraft said that academy lecturers and students were responsible for what was one of the world's largest-scale recordings, with almost 400 tracks that ran continuously for six hours during performances by Shakira, Black Eyed Peas and others at the FIFA Kick-Off concert in 2010. Staff and students also managed the broadcast mix and recording during the Nelson Mandela 46664 concert at Ellis Park.<br/><br/>"Because of the shift in technology, the world of sound, film and video have come together to share common platforms," he said.<br/><br/>"Although the equipment has become easier to use at a basic level, the implementation of this technology at higher levels has become much more complex and technical, hence the need to produce professionals with the technical abilities that the industry demands."<br/><br/>Nigerian performer John Awe made his way to South Africa after he could find no higher education institution with a course in his chosen field in his home country.<br/><br/>"Knowing that ASE's campus is in the SABC building (in Auckland Park) and that we are using the very latest technology makes me confident that I'm studying at a world-class institution," he said. | Predarea Ministerului Sunetului<br/><br/>Bachelor of Science de trei ani se va concentra pe știința ingineriei sunetului și muzicii.<br/><br/>Programul, acreditat de Consiliul pentru Învățământul Superior, a fost conceput în conformitate cu programele de inginerie tehnologică oferite de universitățile publice.<br/><br/>Studenții care doresc să devină profesioniști în industria audio tehnică vor obține o pregătire suficientă în domeniul științei audio, designului și sintezei pentru a produce dezvoltări tehnice inovatoare în domeniu.<br/><br/>MD Tim Kraft, Academia a declarat: "Încă din prima zi în campus, elevii sunt expuși industriei de divertisment și interacționează zilnic cu profesioniștii din industrie.
