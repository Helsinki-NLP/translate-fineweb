Third, teachers need to know about hardware components. Fourth, teachers must know about available software and its compatibility with hardware. The fifth area in which teachers must be knowledgeable is in microcomputer applications in the social studies. Teachers can also use computers in the management of classroom tasks. And, finally, teachers must know about the social implications of microcomputers.
